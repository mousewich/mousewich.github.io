Terms of Service

Last Revised June 29, 2024

By commissioning me for any work, you are agreeing to the terms of service outlined below.

General

  • I have the right to decline any commission request for any reason
  • Usage of my work with any NFT, AI system, or related future technology is strictly prohibited.
  • I will not accept work for clients younger than 18 years of age.
  • The terms outlined here may vary for certain commissions, in which case the adjusted terms will be negotiated directly with the client.

Payment

  • Payment will be handled through a PayPal invoice in USD.
  • Payment is required in full after discussion and acceptance of the price of the work. Work will not begin prior to payment.
  • Pricing examples provided on this website and elsewhere are estimates and the exact price of each commission will be dependent on the complexity of the work and quoted independently.

Communication and Delivery

  • Communication will take place primarily through email.
  • Failure to communicate by myself or the client for 7 days will result in a cancellation of the commission.
  • Deadlines must be agreed upon prior to the start of a commission. Rush fees may be negotiated for work to be delivered on a strict deadline.
  • I will communicate any anticipated delays and negotiate refunds or other applicable remedies with the client in a timely matter.
  • The delivery of work will depend on the nature of the commission. Digital files will primarily be delivered through email.

Revisions

  • Revisions to the work may be made at any time throughout the commission process prior to the acceptance of the final product by the client.
  • Minor revisions or revisions made due to my own negligence are free of charge. Otherwise, major revisions to the work may incur an additional charge.
  • Revision charges will be negotiated with the client prior to the revision(s) being made.
  • The distinction between major and minor revisions is made solely at my discretion.

Copyright and Usage

  • I retain all rights over any commissioned works.
  • Unless otherwise agreed upon, all commissioned work is for personal use only.
  • Commercial licenses will require additional payment on top of the price of the commission.
  • I reserve the right to present the commissioned work on social media or elsewhere as an example of my work or portfolio piece.
  • Clients may inquire about Non-Disclosure Agreements which will require additional payment.
  • Clients must provide clear credit to me as the creator of any commissioned work.
  • Clients may not resell or redistribute commissioned work without prior authorization.

Cancellation and Refunds

  • Once the final product is approved by the client, the work cannot be refunded.
  • The client may choose to cancel the commission at any time. If work has not begun on the commission, a refund will be issued for the full amount paid by the client.
  • I do not issue cancellation refunds for work that has already begun except as outlined in some individual commission agreements.
  • I reserve the right to cancel any commission at my own discretion, at which point a partial refund may be issued based on the commission and work completed.

Retention of Digital Files

  • Clients may make copies and maintain backups of files. I encourage clients to maintain backups of any files delivered as part of a commission.
  • For as long as I have backups of files, I will send them to clients free of charge.
  • I will retain backups of all intermediate and delivered files for a minimum period of 6 months, after which I have no obligation to maintain backups or provide new copies to the client.
